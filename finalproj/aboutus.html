Our Mission


Located in downtown Lutsen, MN, Clearview General strives
for customer satisfaction. We work hard to provide items at a
reasonable price! We serve everything local and fresh from the
surrounding businesses. Here at Clearview General we provide
24 hour gas, serve live bait/tackle, DNR Licenses, fur pelts,
food/beverages, automotive and much more! Stop in today and
be greeted by a warm Northern Minnesota welcome!
